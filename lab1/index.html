    The construction company "CottageHouse" successfully works in the market of construction services in Kiev and Ukraine since 2000. Since then for the second decade we have been realizing the dreams of our clients - we design and build their cozy family nests. Whether it's an apartment, a private house, a dacha or an office space - the range of activities of "CottageHouse" is not limited to simple templates - we find an individual approach to each object.

    A full range of services for the construction of houses "on a turn-key basis", the restoration of residential and non-residential premises, facade and interior finishing works - this and much more specialists of our company perform always in the most professional and, most importantly, within the agreed time.

    A few words about our team. "CottageHouse" is a union of true professionals and craftsmen, where each of us has the appropriate profile training, and therefore we have no doubts about competence to our clients!

    Many years of experience in the range of building issues allows "CottageHouse" to be one of the flagships of the construction market. We are always ready to answer for the result, and we undertake to implement any ideas, no matter how improbable they might seem at first glance. Our competent specialists are always ready to help you in choosing a finished project at home, or individually develop an exclusive project.
